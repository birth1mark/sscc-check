Frequently Asked Questions

What does SSCC stand for?
Serial Shipping Container Code. It is an 18 digit number used to uniquely identify logistics units.

How is SSCC related to GS1?
The SSCC is a core GS1 standard for logistics. It uses a GS1 Company Prefix to ensure global uniqueness.
